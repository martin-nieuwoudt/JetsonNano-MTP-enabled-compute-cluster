### Can you scrub/repair it?

**Technically yes, but not recommended for your use case:**

| Option | Feasibility | Risk |
|--------|-------------|------|
| **`fsck -y` on unmounted root** | Requires booting from another SD/USB (can't repair mounted root) | Medium — may not fix all metadata corruption |
| **`mkfs.ext4` (reformat)** | 100% fixes filesystem repair | **Destroys all data** — you'd lose the compiled binaries anyway |
| **`dd` clone to new card** | Clones corruption | Propagates errors to new card |
